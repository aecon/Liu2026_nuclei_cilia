# Ilastik classifiers


Three pixel classifiers were trained in ilastik:

1. Classifier 1 was trained on images of the `NBH-HPI-4` and `RML6-HPI-4` groups.  
2. Classifier 2 was trained on images of the `NBH-CentrinoneB` and `NBH-DMSO` groups.  
3. Classifier 3 was trained on images of the `RML6-CentrinoneB and `RML6-DMSO` groups.
